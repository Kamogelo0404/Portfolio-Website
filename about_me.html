About Me

My name is Kamogelo Precious Nkadimeng. I am 22 years of age, I am from a small village
in Limpopo called Elandskraal. I attended school there from Primary until Secondary. During my
childhood, I was a very clever girl, who enjoyed going to school and playing rugby. My dream was to become a
Climatologist, because of the love I had for geography and also future climate expectations.

But that was my only dream before I knew about Software Development. In 2017, I matriculated
in Kubela Secondary School. Things were very hard but I managed to progress and obtained Admission to Bachelor's Dregree.
In 2018 I went to university, I enrolled in Bsc in Mathematical and Computer Sciences. I did not really know about
what Computer Science is, but I knew that it is a very good field of study with lot of life opportunities.

Studying Computer Science helped me to know about Software Development. It is not an easy field
to accomplish but I worked hard to secure my future. I now hold a qualification in Mathematical Science,
majored in Computer Science and Statistics, studied from the University of Limpopo. I love Software Dev because it is a
continuous learning process as technology keeps on evolving and I learn new skills everyday. It also provides me a
chance to help other people through the power of technology. My goal is to grow more in the field of technology.
